Our Engagement Models

We at IConflux combine ambitious thinking with practical implementation. Our business models map the best path for your needs while driving digital innovation and generating traction.

Time & Material Model

For the Project which is undefined & ongoing and needs technical consultation before moving to development. This is suitable for projects of all sizes, whether small, mid-sized, or enterprise-level

  • Flexible for scale up or down team as per business needs & Requirements
  • Work can be done on single or multiple projects
  • Complete control over Project Costing
  • Daily Standup Meeting and Reporting
  • Most suitable for changing requirements
  • Agile Development & Implementation

Dedicated Team Model

For more attention on the project and need a dedicated team for quick development. The offshore team is a cost-effective & hassle-free Option for CXOs looking to work closely on the project.

  • Highest cost advantages and ROI of the investment
  • Expansion of technical vertices to find best Developers
  • Access to skilled professionals
  • Increased focus on core activities
  • Risk mitigation
  • Ideal for enterprise-level projects

Fix Cost Model

For projects where the scope is clearly defined and iterations aren't needed. This is primarily suitable for projects ranging from small to midsize.

  • Suited for Clear, Stable & well Defined Requirements
  • Team allocation depending on size of project.
  • Milestone wise sprint Demo & Payment Structure
  • End to End Project Delivery on fixed cost basis
